What is BTRIPS?

NFT marketplace platform for Collectibles & luxury goods

BTRIPS is a model that links luxury products with NFT and provides a marketplace that enables auction, sales, and lucky draw.(with donation)

BTRIPS Token used for bidding purchase and subscription will be burned and therefore excluded from the distribution amount.
And the collected BTRIPS Tokens will be donated to designated organizations except for the minimum operation cost.

Getting Started



Start trading various NFTs on BTRIPS Marketplace.

Users may purchase luxury fashion items, participate in auction or enter the lucky draw to win memorabilia items of celebrities and influencers.

Buyers may redeem real assets at BTRIPS offline stores upon submission of the matching NFTs.



Store tokens safe, exchange them at once.

The BTRIPS mobile application provides every function you need to manage digital assets such as token wallet, token exchange and security system.
